Section 7 — How It Works

Long hair in four ridiculously easy steps.

Step Image Choosing a kit
1

Choose your kit

Pick your length (16", 20" or 24") and whether you're after straight/wavy or curly. Not sure? We're a phone call away.

Step Image Taping a weft
2

Tape it up

Apply your new extensions using our included double-sided extension tape. Takes about 25 minutes all up.

Step Image Pressing in weft
3

Press down, let down

Section your hair, press in each weft close to the roots, and let your natural hair fall over them. That's it. Go be gorgeous.

Step Image Removal spray
4

Remove, wash, repeat

Every 3–4 weeks, use the included removal spray, wash, re-tape, and go again. Your kit lasts 12–18 months.
